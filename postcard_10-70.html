Zodiac Postcard October 5, 1970

Bruce Davis' Birthday is October 5

Front of Postcard. Note the 13 holes.  Sent one month after the disappearance of Donna Lass.
 

Back of Postcard. Note the cross drawn in human blood.  Also note the  inverted words.  This is considered a "charm" or "spell" in witchcraft.  In this case it means "I'm crack proof".  The 2/10/69 paste-up to the Sacramento Bee also had inverted writing.
