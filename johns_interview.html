The Kathleen Johns
Interview 

 

 An exclusive interview with the only Zodiac victim to escape unharmed! Hear Ms. Johns' startling account of how she survived the abduction. 

Detective J. Smith and Dr. Howard  Davis  were privileged to interview Kathleen Johns on New Years Day, 1998. She is the "woemen" (sic) Zodiac referred to in his July 24, 1970 missive: "This is the Zodiac speaking. I am rather unhappy because you people (North Bay) will not wear some nice (Zodiac) buttons. So I now have a little list, starting with the woeman (sic) (Kathleen) and her baby (Jennifer) that I gave a rather interesting ride for a coupple (sic) howers (sic) one evening a few months back (3/22/70) that ended in my burning her car where (in the area) I found them."

You can read about this incident in author Robert Graysmith's bestseller "Zodiac" and also in "The Zodiac /  Manson Connection." If it was the Zodiac that Ms. Johns escaped from (as some researchers believe) she is the only Zodiac victim to escape unharmed and as Graysmith says, ". . . lived to tell about it"!

This fascinating and one of a kind interview with Kathleen Johns takes place in a restaurant, but despite some background noise, is overall very clear. In the interview you will hear facts that lead us to believe she was definitely a Zodiac victim.

You will hear startling information from the only person who escaped from the Zodiac Killer unharmed. 

We were sorry to hear that Kathleen Johns passed away from a massive heart attack in April 2002. We firmly believe that she was abducted by Zodiac and given, as Zodiac said, "a rather interesting ride." Though she has left this life her testimony remains.
