History of The Zodiac / Manson Connection Website
 

This timeline is a work in progress. If and when more information becomes available it will be included but it is likely some facts were never documented. The dates of much of this history and most of the <Screen Shots> (click on each one for a visual history) were obtainable thanks to Internet Archive's Wayback Machine.
 

On the <Screen Shot> pages scroll to the bottom of each page

and click on Back to return to where you left the timeline.

1999-04-13

–

ZodiacMurders.com Domain is established. At the very first the site was only one page with the introduction on it and Jim Nelson's address for ordering the book. Webgraphixx first designed and maintained the site. The logo was in a red and black motif with all other lettering being white with some yellow on a solid red
background. It stayed this way until at least 2000-11-09.

2001-04-01 –

By this date the first pages of the site had been established and the main portion of the page had been changed to white lettering on a black background. The characters Zodiac used in his cryptograms became the background theme for the
   
site. There had been 2,323 "hits" on the site.

<Screen Shot>
2001-04-22 –

The ZodiacMurders.com website was not being actively updated or maintained. So around this date is when Jim Nelson took over being Webmaster / Editor of the site. At some time between the previous date and this one the victims.htm page went online with its links to the new victim_bates.htm, victim_bennedict.htm and  victim_johns.htm pages. And there were a few other new pages. These were some of the first additions to the site made by Nelson during his renovation.

By this date the site had grown to approximately 25 pages.
