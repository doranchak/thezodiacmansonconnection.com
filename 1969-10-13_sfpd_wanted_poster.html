October 13, 1969 SFPD Wanted Poster
 

 This is the SFPD's wanted poster for the Zodiac issued two days after the Paul Stine murder in San Francisco. Note carefully the suspect's description. It is a description of Bruce Davis and was given long before the behavior of revisionism took over.

 

 

Courtesy of Sandy Betts who has this original poster.
 

 

This may be the sharpest image of the 10/13/69 wanted poster sketch available.
