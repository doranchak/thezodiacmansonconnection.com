Donna Lass - Donna Lass was a pretty twenty-five year old woman with frosted blonde hair and blue eyes. She worked as a nurse at the Sahara hotel in Stateline, Nevada and was by all accounts a "good girl." On Labor Day, September 6, 1970 Donna Lass disappeared and some believe she was abducted from where she worked by the Zodiac killer . . .
