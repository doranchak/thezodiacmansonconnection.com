The Nineteen Spaces

  

 

  

 

 

  

Zodiac held to his word when he stated, "I will not give you my name," and in another letter "No address" (indicating that he was a transient.) The above shows only that he possibly was giving another unsolvable clue to his name by typing his name using spaces for the actual characters. This was pointed out by an instructor friend of the author's when she was told that the line after "BY" was not long enough to accommodate the 18 characters of Davis' full name. She observed that this was an optical illusion and suggested using a ruler to mark off the spaces.
